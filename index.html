THE NATIONAL COLLEGE

This post is written by Dr. P.L. Ramesh

The National Degree College, Jayanagar, Bangalore, an autonomous college, is one of the fifteen institutions, being managed by the National Education Society of Karnataka. The Society was established in 1917 with nationalist ideals as the driving force. The College was started in 1965 with a humble strength of 110 in the Pre- University Course and now it has grown into a leading institution of higher learning in General Education. The College has several achievements to its credit in the academic field as well as extracurricular activities.

ABOUT COLLEGE

This post is written by Dr. P.L. Ramesh

The B.V. Jagadeesh Science Centre established in 2002 aims at popularizing the Basic Sciences among students from this College and from other educational institutions as well. The College offers Under Graduate courses in the streams of B.A., B.Sc., B.C.A. & B.Com. and Post Graduate Courses in Physics, Mathematics and Commerce. From the academic year 2019-20 the College is offering 3 New Tech-Based Courses such as B.C.A. (Internet of Things), B.C.A. (Data Science) and B.Sc. (Bio-Medical Electronics). Moreover for the benefit of students the College conducts Add-On courses and various Certificate courses. The College has a pro-active Placement Cell conducting Soft Skill Programmes and Career Counseling Programmes that have empowered the students to be selected for jobs in reputed companies. The institution has well equipped laboratories, a good digital library and sprawling sports field that have contributed to holistic education and complete Personality Development of students.
